CELL GAMES SAGA

The Cell Games Saga is a pivotal arc in "Dragon Ball Z," where the bio-engineered android Cell, having achieved his perfect form, challenges Earth's strongest fighters to a deadly tournament. Goku, Gohan, and the Z Fighters face off against Cell in a series of intense battles, with Gohan ultimately ascending to Super Saiyan 2 and defeating Cell in a dramatic showdown.
