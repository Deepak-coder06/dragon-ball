FRIEZA SAGA

The Frieza Saga in *Dragon Ball Z* follows Goku and his friends as they battle the tyrannical Frieza on Planet Namek. This arc is pivotal as it showcases Goku's transformation into a Super Saiyan, a legendary warrior state that drastically enhances his power. The saga is intense and emotional, culminating in an epic showdown that solidifies Goku's role as Earth's greatest defender.
