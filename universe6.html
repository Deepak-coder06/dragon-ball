UNIVERE 6 SAGA

The Universe 6 Saga in *Dragon Ball Super* introduces a thrilling tournament between Universe 7, led by Goku and Vegeta, and Universe 6, led by Champa. The saga showcases the introduction of powerful new characters like Hit and Cabba, bringing fresh dynamics to the series. The battles are intense, filled with strategic combat and unexpected twists, culminating in Goku's fight with the legendary assassin, Hit.
