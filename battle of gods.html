BATTLE OF GODS SAGA

The **Battle of Gods Saga** marks the beginning of "Dragon Ball Super" and introduces Beerus, the God of Destruction, who awakens after decades in search of the Super Saiyan God. Goku, after learning about this form, undergoes a ritual to achieve the Super Saiyan God transformation and battles Beerus to protect Earth.
