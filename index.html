DRAGON BALL FRANCHISE

The *Dragon Ball* franchise, created by Akira Toriyama, is a globally beloved series that began as a manga in 1984 and expanded into numerous anime series, movies, and video games. It follows the adventures of Goku and his friends as they protect Earth and the universe from powerful foes, blending action, humor, and heartfelt moments. Known for its iconic transformations, intense battles, and enduring themes of friendship and perseverance, *Dragon Ball* has become a cultural phenomenon, inspiring generations of fans worldwide...,
