UNIVERSAL SURVIVAL SAGA

The Universal Survival Saga in *Dragon Ball Super* is a high-stakes arc where Goku and his allies face off against powerful warriors from across the multiverse in the Tournament of Power. The fate of multiple universes hangs in the balance, with the losing teams facing complete annihilation. This saga showcases intense battles, new transformations, and unexpected alliances, highlighting the resilience and determination of Goku and his team.
