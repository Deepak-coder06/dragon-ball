MAJIN BUU SAGA

The Majin Buu Saga is the final arc of "Dragon Ball Z," where the Z Fighters face the ancient and powerful villain, Majin Buu. The saga features intense battles, dramatic transformations, including Goku's Super Saiyan 3, and the fusion of Goku and Vegeta into Vegito. Ultimately, Buu is defeated by a Spirit Bomb powered by the Earth's energy, leading to a peaceful resolution and the series' conclusion.
