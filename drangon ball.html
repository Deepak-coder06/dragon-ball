DRAGON BALL

*Dragon Ball* is the original anime series that follows the young Saiyan Goku as he embarks on his journey to find the mystical Dragon Balls, which grant any wish when all seven are collected. The series combines martial arts, humor, and adventure, introducing iconic characters like Bulma, Master Roshi, and Krillin, while exploring Goku's growth from a naive boy to a skilled martial artist. With its charming blend of action and comedy, *Dragon Ball* laid the foundation for the epic stories that would follow in the franchise....,
