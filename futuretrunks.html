FUTURE TRUNKS SAGA

The Future Trunks Saga in *Dragon Ball Super* revolves around Trunks' return from a dystopian future where Earth has been devastated by a powerful foe known as Goku Black. As Trunks seeks help from Goku and Vegeta, they uncover the dark truth behind Goku Black's origins, leading to an intense battle across time and space. This saga explores themes of hope, despair, and the consequences of tampering with time, culminating in an epic showdown that tests the limits of their power.
